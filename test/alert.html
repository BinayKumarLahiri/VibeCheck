Alert

The sudden, sharp crack of a twig behind her instantly snapped her into a state of heightened vigilance. Every sense was instantly on high alert; her ears strained to catch the slightest repetition of the sound, and her gaze scanned the dense, darkening woods. Her body tensed slightly, not in fear, but in a controlled state of readiness, a primal instinct taking over her focus. She moved with deliberate, silent caution, her mind calculating the direction and potential source of the noise, focused entirely on situational awareness and self-preservation.
