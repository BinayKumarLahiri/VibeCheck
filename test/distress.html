Distress

The news of the accident sent a shockwave of overwhelming suffering through her, collapsing her against the wall in a state of profound distress. Tears streamed down her face, deep, wrenching sobs that left her gasping for air. She felt utterly helpless, unable to process the magnitude of the pain and the sheer terror of the unknown future. Her entire being was consumed by an unbearable agony, leaving her completely shattered and desperate for relief from the crushing emotional weight.
