Bored

He slumped deeper into the chair, the clock hand ticking with an agonizing slowness that seemed to mock his state of utter apathy. The lecture droned on, a monotonous river of facts that completely failed to capture his attention, leaving him feeling deeply bored. His foot tapped a sporadic rhythm against the floor, a restless, unfulfilled energy searching for any mental escape. He felt a frustrating dissatisfaction, a yearning for a challenge or a stimulation that this environment simply refused to provide.
