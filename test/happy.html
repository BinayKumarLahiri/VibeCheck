Happy

The moment the final exam score flashed on the screen, a wave of pure joy washed over her, making her heart feel light and buoyant. She let out a little cheer, a bright, spontaneous sound that was purely happy. Walking out of the building, her steps had an extra spring, and a genuine, easy smile stretched across her face. This feeling of satisfaction and well-being was infectious; she felt an overwhelming sense of gratitude for all her hard work paying off. Everything in the world seemed momentarily brighter, clearer, and full of wonderful promise.
