Calm

She sat by the window, watching the rain fall, her mind clear and peacefully tranquil. There was a quiet composure in her posture, a complete lack of agitation despite the swirling chaos of the week's problems. She felt deeply calm, her breathing even and slow, her thoughts moving with a measured, deliberate clarity. This serene state wasn't achieved by avoiding her thoughts, but by accepting them without reaction, finding a solid, unshakable core of emotional stability within herself.
