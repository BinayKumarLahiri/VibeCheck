Excited

The countdown clock for the launch party was down to a single minute, and a powerful surge of energy made him bounce on the balls of his feet. This thrilling anticipation for the event had been building all week, manifesting as a swarm of butterflies in his stomach that were now doing aggressive laps. His eyes were wide and shining, constantly flicking between the massive stage and the clock, eager for the first blast of music. He felt almost euphoric, unable to contain the boundless enthusiasm for what was about to unfold.
