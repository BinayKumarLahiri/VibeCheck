Relaxed

Sinking into the warm bubble bath, she let out a long sigh, feeling the accumulated tension of the day melt away from her shoulders and neck. A pervasive sense of physical ease spread through her limbs, leaving her feeling utterly relaxed and wonderfully heavy. She closed her eyes, mind slowing to a pleasant drift, utterly free from worry or the need to rush. It was a moment of complete repose, a much-needed mental and physical unwinding that allowed her to simply be in the moment.
