Tense

He sat rigidly at his desk, shoulders pulled tight, the muscles in his neck aching from the sustained strain of the deadline looming over him. The air in the room felt thick with unspoken pressure, and his jaw was involuntarily clenched, a physical manifestation of his internal struggle. He was profoundly tense, unable to mentally relax or switch off as his brain obsessively reviewed every potential error in the report. This pervasive nervousness made every movement feel stiff and every minor distraction an enormous irritation.
